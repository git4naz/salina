Our Story

Salina began as a labor of love for Executive Chef and Head Mixologist, Caty H. Song. After growing up working her family’s multi-generational farm in Salina, Kansas, Chef Song carries on an abiding love for the natural ingredients and sensibility of the area. Following her years of award-winning barbeque in Kansas and over 15 years of study and innovative mixology in New York City, Chef Song married the two to create the ultimate dining experience. Three-time winner of the James Beard Award, this balanced blend of barbeque and cocktails can’t be beat. We welcome you home in Salina.
